FAQ

How do I restore my purchase?
Open Cloudless, go to Settings or the unlock screen and tap Restore Purchases.

How do I cancel my subscription?
iPhone Settings, tap your name, Subscriptions, Cloudless, Cancel.

Where is my data stored?
Everything stays on your device. We collect nothing.
