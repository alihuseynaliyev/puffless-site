Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Puffless does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • All your data — profile, streaks, craving logs, check-ins, lessons and coach conversations — is stored locally on your device only.
  • The AI coach runs entirely on-device. Your conversations never leave your phone.
  • We use no analytics SDKs, no advertising trackers, and no third-party services that receive your data.
  • Purchases are processed by Apple. We never see or store your payment information.
  • Notifications are scheduled locally on your device.
  • Deleting the app deletes all of your data with it.

Because we collect nothing, there is nothing for us to share, sell, or lose.
